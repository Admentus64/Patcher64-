Patch Options for Link's Awakening:
1. Apply Patch (requires a patch file)
2. Redux and Additional Options

Known Issues:
- Unknown

Requirements:
- US/PAL GBC ROM for Link's Awakening

Instructions:
- Select GBC ROM
- Select patch
- Enable optional "Enable Redux" or "Enable Options" checkboxes
- Customize your patch within "Select Options"
- Press "Patch Selected Options"

Information:
- Original GBC ROM is preserved
    
Programs:
- Floating IPS
