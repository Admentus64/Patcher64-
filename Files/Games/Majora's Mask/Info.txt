Patches for Majora's Mask:
1. ROM Injection (requires .Z64 ROM)
2. Free Patching (requires .IPS, .BPS, .XDELTA, .VCDIFF or .PPF File)
3. Two ROM hacks (MM Redux, Masked Quest)
4. Two fan translations (Polish, Russian)

Known Issues:
- Unknown

Requirements (one of):
- The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask US VC WAD
- The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask US 1.0 Z64 ROM

Instructions:
- Select VC WAD or Z64 ROM
- Select patch
- Enable optional VC Options checkboxes
- Enable optional "Enable Redux" or "Enable Options" checkboxes
- Customize your patch within "Select Options" and "Select Language"
- Press "Patch Select Option"

Documentation:
- https://github.com/ShadowOne333/Zelda64-Redux-Documentation

Information:
- Original Z64 ROM or VC WAD is preserved
- Patches are mostly compatible with existing AR/Gecko Codes
- Redux forces Remap D-Pad 
- Expand Memory renders AR/Gecko Codes unsuable
