Patches for Ocarina of Time:
1. ROM Injection (requires .Z64 ROM)
2. Free Patching (requires .IPS, .BPS, .XDELTA, .VCDIFF or .PPF File)
3. Two ROM hacks (OoT Redux, Dawn and Dusk)
4. Four fan translations (Spanish, Polish, Russian, Chinese)

Known Issues:
- Trees show transparent outlines through walls (Dawn and Dusk)

Requirements (one of):
- The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time US VC WAD
- The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time US 1.0 Z64 ROM

Instructions:
- Select VC WAD or Z64 ROM
- Select patch
- Enable optional VC Options checkboxes
- Enable optional "Enable Redux" or "Enable Options" checkboxes
- Customize your patch within "Select Options" and "Select Language"
- Press "Patch Select Option"

Documentation:
- https://github.com/ShadowOne333/Zelda64-Redux-Documentation

Information:
- Original Z64 ROM or VC WAD is preserved
- Certain patches requires the 8MB Expansion Pak to be enabled
- Few patches are compatible with existing AR/Gecko Codes
- Redux forces Expand Memory, Remap D-Pad and Leave D-Pad Up
- Most patches forces Downgrade
    
Programs:
- Wad Packer/Wad Unpacker
- Floating IPS
- PPF3
- Wiimm's 'wszst' Tool
- Compress Tool
- ndec Tool
- TabExt Tool
- RN64CRC
