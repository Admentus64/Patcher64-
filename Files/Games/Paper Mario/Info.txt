Patches for Paper Mario:
1. ROM Injection (requires .Z64 ROM)
2. Free Patching (requires .IPS, .BPS, .XDELTA, .VCDIFF or .PPF File)
3. Paper Mario: Hard Mode (Extra damage)
4. Paper Mario: Hard Mode+ (Extra damage and enemy HP) 
5. Paper Mario: Insane Mode (Insane damage)

Known Issues:
- Unknown

Requirements (one of):
- Paper Mario US VC WAD
- Paper Mario US Z64 ROM

Instructions:
- Select VC WAD or Z64 ROM
- Select patch
- Enable optional VC Options checkboxes
- Press "Patch Select Option"

Information:
- Original ROM or WAD is preserved
- Existing AR/Gecko codes still work
