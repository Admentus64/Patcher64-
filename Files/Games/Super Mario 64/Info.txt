Patches for Super Mario 64:
1. ROM Injection (requires .Z64 ROM)
2. Free Patching (requires .IPS, .BPS, .XDELTA, .VCDIFF or .PPF File)
3. Super Mario 64: 60 FPS v2 (Native 60 FPS support)
4. Super Mario 64: Free Cam (Analog Camera)
5. SM64: Multiplayer (V1.4.2)

Known Issues:
- Mario camera is inoperable (60 FPS)
- Intro demo is broken (60 FPS)

Requirements (one of):
- Super Mario 64 US VC WAD
- Super Mario 64 US Z64 ROM

Instructions:
- Select VC WAD or Z64 ROM
- Select patch
- Enable optional VC Options checkboxes
- Enable optional "Enable Options" checkboxes
- Customize your patch within "Select Options"
- Press "Patch Select Option"

Information:
- Original Z64 ROM or VC WAD is preserved
- Star Road and Multiplayer include a modified VC emulator
- Existing AR/Gecko codes still work (60 FPS / Analog Camera)
- Existing AR/Gecko codes do not work (Multiplayer)
- Enable second emulated controller (Analog Camera / Multiplayer)
- Bind second emulated Control Stick to primary physical controller
     
Programs:
- Wad Packer/Wad Unpacker
- Floating IPS
- Xdelta3
- Wiimm's 'wszst' Tool
- sm64extend Tool
