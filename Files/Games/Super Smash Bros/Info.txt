Patches for Super Smash Bros:
1. ROM Injection (requires .Z64 ROM)
2. Free Patching (requires .IPS, .BPS, .XDELTA, .VCDIFF or .PPF File)
3. Super Smash Bros. Remix (new characters and stages)
4. Super Smash Bros: More Stage Edition (new stages)
5. Widescreen

Known Issues:
- Training Mode crashes (Widescreen patch for Virtual Console)

Requirements (one of(:
- Super Smash Bros. US VC WAD
- Super Smash Bros. US Z64 ROM

Instructions:
- Select VC WAD or Z64 ROM
- Select patch
- Customize Virtual Console options
- Press "Patch Select Options"

Information:
- Original ROM or WAD is preserved
- Remix requires the 8MB Expansion Pak to be enabled
