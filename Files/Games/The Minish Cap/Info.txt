Patch Options for The Minish Cap:
1. Apply Patch (requires a patch file)
2. Redux and Additional Options

Known Issues:
- Unknown

Requirements:
- US or PAL GBA ROM for The Minish Cap

Instructions:
- Select GBA ROM
- Select patch
- Enable optional "Enable Options" checkbox
- Customize your patch within "Select Options"
- Press "Patch Selected Options"

Information:
- Original GBA ROM is preserved
    
Programs:
- Floating IPS
